Are you in need of a warm, comforting, and nutritious meal? Look no further than this Vegan Lentil Soup recipe! Packed with healthy ingredients like lentils, carrots, and celery, this soup is not only delicious but also good for you.

Lentils are a great source of protein and fiber, and they add a satisfying texture to this soup. The addition of carrots and celery adds not only extra nutrients but also a hint of sweetness and crunch to the soup.

This recipe is simple and straightforward, making it the perfect meal for busy weeknights. And, if you have any leftovers, it reheats nicely for lunch the next day. So, let's get started!

Ingredients:
1 cup green or brown lentils
3 carrots, diced
2 celery stalks, diced
1 onion, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
6 cups vegetable broth
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper, to taste
##Instructions:

In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
Add the onion, carrots, and celery and cook until the vegetables are soft and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
Stir in the lentils and vegetable broth.
Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes or until the lentils are tender.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve hot with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, if desired.
Enjoy this hearty and nutritious Vegan Lentil Soup with Carrots and Celery! Whether you're looking for a warm meal to chase away the chill or a comforting bowl of goodness, this recipe is sure to hit the spot.
