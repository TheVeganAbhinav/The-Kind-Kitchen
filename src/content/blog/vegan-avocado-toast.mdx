Good morning! Are you tired of the same old breakfast routine? Looking for a quick, nutritious and delicious way to start your day? Look no further! Today, we’re sharing a vegan version of the classic avocado toast, elevated with juicy tomatoes and crunchy sprouts.

This vegan avocado toast is the perfect blend of flavors and textures. The creamy and smooth avocado is complemented by the juicy sweetness of the ripe tomatoes, while the crunchy sprouts add a burst of freshness. And the best part? It’s incredibly easy to make!

Here’s what you’ll need:

2 slices of whole grain bread
1 ripe avocado
1 ripe tomato
2 handfuls of sprouts
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional: lemon juice, hot sauce, or any other toppings of your choice
Instructions:

Toast your bread until it’s nice and crispy.
Slice your avocado in half, remove the pit, and mash it with a fork. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Spread the mashed avocado on your toasted bread.
Slice your tomato and add it on top of the avocado.
Sprinkle the sprouts on top of the tomato.
Drizzle with lemon juice and hot sauce, if desired.
And that’s it! Your vegan avocado toast with tomato and sprouts is ready to be enjoyed. This breakfast is not only delicious, but also packed with nutrients to help you start your day on the right foot. The avocado provides healthy fats and fiber, while the tomato and sprouts add vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to your diet.

So why not give this vegan avocado toast a try? You won’t be disappointed! Enjoy!
