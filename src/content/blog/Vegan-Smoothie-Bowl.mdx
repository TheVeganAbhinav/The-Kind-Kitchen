Good morning! Are you looking for a delicious and healthy breakfast option? Look no further! Today, we are going to make a vegan smoothie bowl that will fuel your day and keep you satisfied until lunch.

A smoothie bowl is a thick smoothie that is served in a bowl and topped with granola, fresh fruits, and other tasty toppings. This vegan smoothie bowl is made with a blend of non-dairy milk, frozen berries, and silken tofu, which provides a creamy and smooth texture without the addition of dairy.

Ingredients:

1 cup non-dairy milk (almond milk, coconut milk, etc.)
1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, etc.)
1/2 block of silken tofu
1 tsp honey (optional)
Granola and fresh fruits for topping (banana, raspberry, blueberry, etc.)
Instructions:

In a blender, add the non-dairy milk, frozen mixed berries, silken tofu, and honey (if using).
Blend until smooth and creamy.
Pour the smoothie mixture into a bowl.
Top with granola and fresh fruits.
Serve and enjoy!
This vegan smoothie bowl is a great way to start your day with a healthy meal that is full of nutrients, vitamins, and antioxidants. The silken tofu adds protein to the smoothie, while the frozen berries provide a natural sweetness and a burst of flavor. The granola and fresh fruits add crunch and texture to the smoothie bowl, making it a satisfying meal that you can enjoy for breakfast, lunch, or even dinner.
